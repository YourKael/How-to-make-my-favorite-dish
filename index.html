Mykael's Recipe for his favourite Chop Suey!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup shortening
  • 1 ½ cups diced pork loin
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can bean sprouts, drained and rinsed
  • ⅓ cup cold water
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar

Instructions

  1. Heat shortening in a large, deep skillet over medium-high meat.
  2. pork and sear until it turns white.
  3. Add onion and sauté for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in celery, hot water, salt, and pepper.
  5. Cover skillet and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Add sprouts and bring mixture to a boil.
  7. Combine cold water, cornstarch, soy sauce, and sugar in a small bowl.
  8. Add to skillet mixture and cook until thickened to desired consistency, about 5 minutes.
